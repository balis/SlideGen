Title: 11% of data employees paste into ChatGPT is confidential
URL: https://www.cyberhaven.com/blog/4-2-of-workers-have-pasted-company-data-into-chatgpt
Retrieved: 2026-05-27

Full excerpt:
"11% of what employees paste into ChatGPT is confidential. 4.7% of employees have pasted confidential data into ChatGPT at least once. 10.8% of knowledge workers have tried using it at least once in the workplace and 8.6% have pasted data into it."

"The average company leaks confidential material to ChatGPT hundreds of times per week."

Most common types of confidential data leaked:
- "Sensitive/internal only data (319 incidents per week per 100,000 employees)"
- "Source code (278)"
- "Client data (260)"

"7,999 attempts to paste corporate data into ChatGPT per 100,000 employees" during peak usage periods.

Note: The Cyberhaven study analysed data from 1.6 million knowledge workers in 2023. A follow-up in March 2024 found 27.4% of corporate data put into AI tools was sensitive, up from 10.7% a year earlier — a 485% increase in volume.
