Excerpt:
Samsung Electronics banned employee use of popular generative AI tools like ChatGPT
after discovering staff uploaded sensitive code to the platform. Within less than 20
days of allowing ChatGPT access, Samsung experienced three separate incidents where
engineers exposed confidential company information:
1. An employee copied source code from a faulty semiconductor database into ChatGPT
   and asked it to find a fix.
2. Another employee submitted an entire meeting transcript and asked the chatbot to
   create meeting minutes.
3. A third used ChatGPT to optimize a test sequence for identifying yield and
   defective chips.

The ban took effect on May 1, 2023. Samsung subsequently announced plans to develop
internal AI systems with proper data controls.
