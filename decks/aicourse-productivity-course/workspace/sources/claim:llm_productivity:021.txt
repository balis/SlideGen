Statement: Planning and organizing is one of the most common use cases for ChatGPT at work.

Source: "How People Are Using ChatGPT" — OpenAI economic research report
URL: https://openai.com/index/how-people-are-using-chatgpt/
Full paper: https://cdn.openai.com/pdf/a253471f-8260-40c6-a2cc-aa93fe9f142e/economic-research-chatgpt-usage-paper.pdf

Excerpt:
ChatGPT usage can be categorized by user intent: "Asking" accounts for about
49% of messages (where people value ChatGPT as an advisor), "Doing" encompasses
40% of usage including task-oriented interactions such as drafting text, planning,
or programming, and "Expressing" captures 11% of usage involving personal
reflection, exploration, and play.

ChatGPT is being used nearly as much for planning as for coding.

Approximately 30% of consumer usage is work-related. The shares are calculated
from a sample of approximately 1.1 million sampled conversations from May 15,
2024 through June 26, 2025. As of July 2025, about 56% of work-related messages
are classified as Doing (e.g., performing job tasks), and nearly three-quarters
of those are Writing tasks.
