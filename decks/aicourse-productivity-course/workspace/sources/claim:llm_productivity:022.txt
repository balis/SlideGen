Excerpt:
With AI, you can turn that mess into a clear set of priorities -- and even
uncover deeper insights you hadn't articulated yet. Popular prompts include:
"Here's my brain dump: [list ideas]. Can you organise them by priority?" and
"Turn this messy list into an action plan with clear next steps".

The Brain Dump to Action Plan technique helps take a messy, overwhelming list
of thoughts and sort them, group tasks into categories for clarity, and identify
the most important actions to focus on first.

When overwhelm hits, you can record for about six minutes about everything in
your head with no structure or filtering, then feed that audio to AI, which
transforms the chaotic rambling into organized, actionable clarity.
