Excerpt:

"A watermark delay (set with withWatermark) of '2 hours' guarantees that the engine will
never drop any data that is less than 2 hours delayed. In other words, any data less than
2 hours behind (in terms of event-time) the latest data processed till then is guaranteed to
be aggregated."

"However, the guarantee is strict only in one direction. Data delayed by more than 2 hours
is not guaranteed to be dropped; it may or may not get aggregated. More delayed is the
data, less likely is the engine going to process it."
